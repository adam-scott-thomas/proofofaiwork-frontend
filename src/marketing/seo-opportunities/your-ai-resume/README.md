# Your AI-Resume SEO Package

Research verdict: build it, but not as another AI resume builder.

The winning position is sharper: the resume is dead; submit your proof. Your AI-Resume is a proof-backed career surface where claims link to AI work samples, receipts, and capability artifacts.

## Live Route Pattern

- Flagship: `/your-ai-resume`
- Campaign alias: `/resume-is-dead`
- Nested suite: `/your-ai-resume/:slug`
- Top-level aliases: each suite slug also resolves at `/:slug`

## Package Files

- `researchVerdict.ts`: strategic verdict and positioning guardrails.
- `clusters.ts`: keyword clusters and intent.
- `pages.ts`: live page definitions.
- `publishingPlan.ts`: 15-day publishing sequence.
- `internalLinks.ts`: internal linking map.
- `pagesToAvoid.ts`: low-value resume SEO variants to avoid.
- `briefs/*.md`: editorial briefs for the suite.
